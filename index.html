FAQ's

1. What would be the Duration of yatra?

Yatra will be for a total of 6 days from Jan 12th to Jan 17th 2020.

2. What is the Mode of transport?

There will be multiple modes of transportation. From Chennai it will be via flight, from base camp it will be via private tourist vehicles. However, those who wish to come via Train are expected to book their tickets well in advance and meet the other yatris at the pre-determined date, time and place. Base camp will be decided and updated shortly.

3. Who will take care of Transport arrangements?

Parasuram Kund Yatra organizing committee will take care of the end to end transportation from Chennai. Those who wish to join us directly at the base camp must make their own arrangements up until that point and back.

4. What's the Accommodation arrangements?

Suitable accommodations are being arranged. This might include (though not limited to) circuit houses, vivekananda kendra schools and community centers. Bedding will be arranged as well.

5. What are the arrangements for Food?

A cook will be accompanying for the entire trip serving basic food and beverages.

6. Are there any Medical fitness requirements or age limit to participate in the yatra?

Age is not a criteria, however fitness certificate is mandatory from a Doctor. Since the temperature is expected to touch 0 to 4 degrees during the time of the year, fitness is essential to participate in the yatra. Enough winter clothing is required, we shall communicate the winter clothing details later via email.

7. Can I bring my family?

Yes, family members can accompany. It is encouraged to come with your spouse and children as it is Punya Theertha Snanam. Those who accompany should also submit a fitness certificate from a doctor.

8. Can I extend my trip and return on my own?

Yes, you can stay back, however the committee will be responsible only till the last date i.e 17th Jan 2020.

9. What would be the cost of the trip?

Flight Charges plus Rs.7500 (over all expenses estimated at Rs 30,000 to 32,000).

Rs.7500 includes Accomadation, Local Transport & Food.

Flight Charges shall be the actual ticket cost as on booking date.

All estimates are based on actual costs. We operate on non-profit model.

10. I have relatives and friends who wish to join from other states in India, can they also register?

Yes, they can register, however they will have to ensure that they join the other Yatris at the base camp at the predetermined date, time and venue. No separate arrangements will be possible for staggered arrivals.

11. Is there an emergency medical help available?

We are working with the local authorities to help us with security and emergency medical support where required. But it is generally advised to carry all your prescription medications.
